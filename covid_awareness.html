About COVID-19

COVID-19 is a respiratory illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It spreads primarily through respiratory droplets and can cause mild to severe symptoms. Protecting yourself and others is essential to limit its spread.

Prevention

  • Wear a mask in crowded or indoor spaces.
  • Wash your hands regularly with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Maintain a safe distance of at least 1.5 meters from others.
  • Avoid touching your face with unwashed hands.
  • Get vaccinated and stay updated with booster doses.

Symptoms

Common symptoms of COVID-19 include:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue
  • Loss of taste or smell

If you experience severe symptoms, such as difficulty breathing or persistent chest pain, seek medical attention immediately.
